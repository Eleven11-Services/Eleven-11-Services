Not all projects begin with a clear-cut brief.Sometimes, it starts with a spark of conversation.That’s exactly how our journey with Travancore Foundation,a serene senior citizen homelocated in the green heart of Karukachal, Kerala, began.

The Turning Point: Show, Don’t Just Tell

To earn their trust, we showcased our previous works - videos we’d produced for brands likeBismi Hypermarket, Tata Motors, Y Mall, Lulu Mall, Royal Drive, and Benz. These weren’tjust videos; they were experiences, captured and told with precision. Travancore Foundationwas impressed. They saw what we were capable of. And just like that, we had a green light.

Now the real work began.

Planning the Shoot: Every Frame Matters

Before we ever hit record, our team sat down and chalked out a comprehensive video direction plan. This included scripting, lighting arrangements, camera angles, and shot sequences. We understood the emotion behind the place, and we needed every frame to reflect that.

To ensure visual excellence, we used a combination of drone shots for aerial views, wide-angle shots for infrastructure, and tight close-ups for emotional depth, particularly during testimonial captures. We also brought in one of our anchors from Eleven:11 to narrate the story in a warm and engaging tone.

Our commitment to video quality is something we never compromise on. To strengthen the final output, we utilized Topaz video enhancement technology, ensuring every frame wascrisp, clear, and cinematic.

Execution: Lights, Camera, Commitment

The entire shoot was completed in just one day, a challenge we embraced with full force. Ourteam worked from dawn to dusk, adjusting lighting, coordinating scenes, and ensuring everything ran like clockwork. We knew the deadline was tight. And we also understood that the client wasn’t deeply familiar with the technical aspects of video production.

So, we took extra care to educate them through the process, explaining the logic behind each decision, making them part of the creative journey.

Overcoming Challenges with Passion

Two major hurdles stood in our way:

A tight delivery timeline

Client’s unfamiliarity with technical nuances
