This case study details our successful partnership with ORB Restobar in Kochi, where our strategic approach transformed its brand image and cemented its positionas a leading destination in Kochi.

Bold Visuals, Elevated Ambience

Our creative posters for Orb Resto Bar in kochi didn’t just grab attention they brought in real results. The client loved the impact, and the audience feedback spoke for itself.

PROBLEM

ORB Restobar, a prominent establishment in Kochi, sought to rebrand its digitalidentity from a typical nightlife and party venue to a preferred family-friendly destination. This required a strategic digital campaign focused on attracting family audiences while maintaining its appeal to younger demographics, withan urgent need for creative assets to support ongoing promotional activities.

The Impact

Brand Perception: ORB successfully established itself as a top-tier restobarin Kochi, gaining significant traction among both young and familyaudiences.

Digital Reach: The creative content achieved lakhs of organic views,demonstrating strong audience engagement.

Audience Growth: The social media follower count saw a remarkableincrease, with a growth of 3,000 to 10,000 followers, reinforcing the brand's new image and broader appeal.

Want your Business to Grow

Eleven 11 Services helped ORB Restobar reposition itself from a nightlife venue to a family-friendly destination through a rapid and creative digital campaign. The strategy included compelling visuals, influencer engagement, and a refined brand narrative. As a result, ORB saw a major boost in brand perception, organic reach, and social media growth
