Case Study Update: Scaling Admissions with Precision

Luminar Technolab x Eleven:11 Services

Client: Luminar Technolab

‍Duration Analyzed: May 2025

‍Services: Google Ads, Performance Max Campaigns, GA4 Integration, GMB Optimization

‍Agency: Eleven:11 Services

The Challenge

Kerala’s premier IT training institute, Luminar Technolab, wanted to:

  • Boost enrollments for Data Science, Python, and Full Stack courses.
  • Target high-intent learners in Kochi, Calicut, Trivandrum.
  • Reduce cost per lead while improving lead quality.
  • Reach tech-savvy audiences both in-state and in major metros like Bengaluru.
  • Hit an internal enrollment target of 60+ Data Science seats (₹60,000 each).

Goals

  • Drive high-converting traffic through digital ads.
  • Optimize Google Ad spend for maximum ROI
  • Use GA4 + CRM insights to enhance targeting
  • Improve GMB (Google Business Profile) engagement.
  • Implement city-specific campaigns for Kochi, Calicut, and Trivandrum.
  • Improve lead quality with keyword intent and funnel retargeting

Strategy Snapshot

Google Business Profile (GMB) Growth

Business Profile Interactions
  • April 2025: 3,110 interactions

  • May 2025: Highest ever — showing upward brand discovery

Directions Requests from Google Maps

  • April 2025: 1,820‍
  • May 2025:2,587 – 42% surge in offline intent.

Calls from Google Search

  • May 2025: 704 direct calls (highest till date)‍

What Worked

✅ City-wise Personalization: Ads like “Best Python Course in Kochi” drove better CTRs


✅ Performance Max Experiments: Split by course and location for precise testing

✅ Enhanced Conversion Tracking: Improved attribution using GA4 + Google Tag Manager


✅ Budget Shifting: Reallocated funds based on keyword ROI and bounce rate metrics


✅ Landing Page Experience: Reduced bounce to 13.9% with strong CTA and social proof

✅ YouTube Remarketing: Re-engaged video viewers across the web with tailored offers

Business Impact

Takeaways

18x ROI from paid ads
Highest-ever GMB interactions, direction requests, and calls
Strong brand recall and audience engagement

Conclusion

This campaign proves how precision targeting, data-backed decisions, and creative localization can turn ₹2L into over ₹36L in revenue. In a cluttered education market, results like these set new benchmarks.
