Kiwi

Kiwis are native to China but are now widely grown in New Zealand and Italy, flourishing in temperate climates. They are packed with vitamin C, aiding in skin health and boosting the immune system.

Coconut

Coconuts are tropical fruits with a hard outer husk, a tough shell, and a deliciously sweet, creamy flesh inside. Coconuts are a rich source of healthy fats, fiber, and they are widely known for their versatility.

Strawberry

Blackberry

Blackberries are primarily grown in temperate regions, especially in North America and Europe. Blackberries are rich in vitamin C, and antioxidants, which can help protect against inflammation and chronic diseases.
